About

Divorce Services Online provides a simple and cost effective way to obtain a divorce within the comfort of your own home from anywhere in Australia.

We understand that the breakdown of a marriage can be a highly stressful and difficult situation. Let our experienced family lawyers manage the divorce process for you.

Unlike other online divorce providers, Divorce Services Online provides a comprehensive service that will take care of your divorce from start to finish including drafting, filing, serving and appearing at Court on your behalf if necessary.

Why use Divorce Services Online?

Avoid the stress of completing complicated Application forms yourself
Save thousands of dollars in legal fees
No need to visit a solicitor
Our team of experienced family lawyers will handle the divorce process on your behalf

Simply complete the online questionnaire and leave the rest up to us.
We will file the application at the Court and make arrangements for your spouse to be served.
We can also attend the Court hearing on your behalf, if required.

Do I Qualify For a Divorce?

Have you been separated for over a year?

Do you have no intention of getting back together?

Is your marriage legally recognised in Australia?

Are you an Australian citizen and/or do you live in Australia?

If you can answer yes to all the above you can begin your divorce proceedings immediately.
